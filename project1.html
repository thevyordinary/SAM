Mission

In recent years, learning has gotten more challenging and competitive, while technology has advanced and improved. Since then, there has been an increase in demand from students for educational answer tools that would be integrated into computers and smartphones. Additionally, the AI-based learning systems that are currently accessible are unable to fully resolve and provide a clear explanation. After fully comprehending user requirements, our goal is to develop new AI-based learning with the most advanced capabilities. Hence, there would be a potential for our team to develop a new technology that will be the most advanced and comprehensive aid for Vietnamese pupils

Motivation

Our distinct high school educational experiences served as our inspiration for working on this project. We found that the latest version of AI-based learning tools, such as QANDA, Dicamon, and PhotoMath, do not allow us the flexibility to enter the questions. Additionally, the algorithms used in those programs only look for previously solved answers and a propensity to interpret input questions incorrectly. Additionally, we examine, evaluate, and then apply into our invention some modern IT trends, particularly integrated AI-assistant features to answer, help with foreign language subjects, and Intelligent Recommendation will offer personalized solutions based on the users' strengths, preferences, and potential improvement areas[1]. Finally, we think that not only pupils but also individuals in need may find our website valuable. Developing the website will require a wide range of skills: web programming, collecting user experience, soft skills, project management and collaboration. The concept of AI-based learning is not new, but our solution will be the most thorough and sophisticated AI-based learning currently on the market to attract potential employers
