III) Project Description

1. Overview

Topic

Due to the increased demand for a new technology that can completely solve many compulsory subjects in school, for instance, Math or Physics, only by scanning, entering files with those problems or even finishing this stage vocally. Furthermore, due to the inability to provide accurate and quick answers in the current existing applications. We aim to develop a single app to assist students with their academic work.

This project aims to develop cutting-edge technology that will only be used by Vietnamese high school students, such as a mobile phone app or other high-tech devices. This application uses the latest technological advancements and artificial intelligence (AI). The end result will include a full justification for every response and every question on every topic taught in high school.

Motivation

Our distinct high school educational experiences served as our inspiration for working on this project. We found that the latest version of AI-based learning tools, such as QANDA, Dicamon, and PhotoMath, do not allow us the flexibility to enter the questions. Additionally, the algorithms used in those programs only look for previously solved answers and a propensity to interpret input questions incorrectly. Additionally, we examine, evaluate, and then apply into our invention some modern IT trends, particularly integrated AI-assistant features to answer, help with foreign language subjects, and Intelligent Recommendation will offer personalized solutions based on the users' strengths, preferences, and potential improvement areas[1]. Finally, we think that not only pupils but also individuals in need may find our website valuable. Developing the website will require a wide range of skills: web programming, collecting user experience, soft skills, project management and collaboration. The concept of AI-based learning is not new, but our solution will be the most thorough and sophisticated AI-based learning currently on the market to attract potential employers.

Landscape

Since this concept has been extremely popular due to the advance in artificial intelligence, we acknowledge that several problems will occur when we choose and develop it. Several significant rivals: QANDA (https://qanda.ai/vi)[2], Dicamon (https://dicamon.vn)[3], PhotoMath (https://photomath.com/en)[4] have created products that are comparable to ours. The QANDA website, developed by Mathpresso Inc., served as the most intelligent to scan math problems and provide detailed solutions. In a similar way, the websites for Dicamon and PhotoMath use the same methods that use optical character recognition to answer the questions. These websites provide numerous options for a single question so that students can select the most appropriate ones as they help students in their educational path. After examining the competitors' advantages: offer a mobile application, a user-friendly interface, and a large answer database. Our main objective is to create an AI assistant to help students in grades 10, 11, and 12. The website will provide consumers a variety of features and functions while upholding a user-friendly interface and designs as a result of our virtual assistant's logical and imaginative training. This has made the project created by our team stand out from the competition in the market.

d. Scope and Limits

"There's no such thing as perfection. You're never finished with a film. You run out of time." -- Peter Jackson, director of 'The Lord of the Rings’ and ‘The Hobbit’ trilogies.

Scope

"Student AI Mentor (SAM)" application, which combines generative AI to solve exercises, should contain basic functions as a normal app with calculation tools, however, it will be enhanced by AI through its ability to “think” of possibilities of the current problems and deploy specific solutions. Furthermore, due to the high demand for solutions for other less popular subjects in schools, more tools are added to SAM, to have a variety of approaches than just typing in questions. ...

According to Vietnam Government, the newest list of subjects required to study in high schools are divided into groups, which are: Compulsory (7): Math, Literature, Foreign Language 1, Physical Education, National Defense and Security Education, Experience and Career Guidance activities, Content Local Education Elective (1): Ethnic Minority Language, Foreign Language 2 5 subjects selected from 3 subject groups (each group chooses at least 1 subject): Social Science: History, Geography, Economic Education and Law Natural science subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology Technology and Arts: Technology, Informatics, Art

We were also having debates, evaluating and coming to the decision that there are minor subjects that are not too difficult to be dependent on study apps, so we get rid of them. The list of remaining subjects for developing SAM contains: Math, Literature, Foreign Language (1 & 2), History, Geography, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Technology, Informatics, Art, Physical Education, Civic Education, National Defense and Security Education.

Based on the list, the tools for collecting input data should be flexible. Camera scanning, Keyboard Typing, Voice Recognition/Tracking, etc., and any tool that we can think of, are suitable, popular, and needed for the difference between subjects, so we decided to have a virtual assistant to help students define what subjects that students need help in and suggest suitable tools to collect data, then use it to collect input data. That virtual assistant is also enhanced by AI in order to learn an individual’s habits, memorize and determine which subjects are frequently asked and improve the response speed from that.

After defining the subject that needs to be worked on, AI will define the type of information needed, head to the proper available online source, collect and give answers, and display them on screen with the way of expression that is frequently used in that subject. For instance: If it is Math, it shows formulas, calculation steps and results with explanations. If it is English, it shows tenses, which grammar is being used, structure in sentences, etc. If it is History, it shows events related to input date and time, what caused that event, who is involved in that event, what is the impact, consequences, casualties, and further details. If it is Art, it shows instructions (photos, videos) to draw, theories, samples, examples, etc.

The desired output is simply proper answers/solutions that help students understand what they are struggling with, with the highest accuracy and efficiency.

Limits

Due to the huge impact of generative AI on every aspect, we have to decide what we mean to solve. In my opinion, a good project is a project that can point out specific problems and specific solutions for only them. The harsh problem of developing a study application with generative AI, we have to ensure the quality of sessions and answers given to be the most accurate. Our team is not considered to be professional, so undergraduate students and above will not be put in our scope due to the fact that our team has a huge concern about the complexity and academics of subjects in universities...

So, our market is high school students. Knowledge and exercises from Grades 10 to 12 are not quite as complex as undergraduate level and above, on the other hand, not as easy as lower grades to complete. This limitation gives us enough time to train the AI model to fit in and it will be able to fully understand the concept of other less popular subjects (besides those related to math) on how questions are made, how they should be answered and how to express them in a proper way. Moreover, training AI is a long-term mission, so it should only concentrate on high school knowledge, for the best outcome.
